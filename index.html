Welcome to your rejection tracker

From unemployment to ununemployment

This page is designed to make your job search feel calmer and clearer. Add each application once, then update it as things progress so you can see the full picture.

1. Add applications

Use the Add New tab to save the role, company, and your initial impression of the fit.

2. Track progress

On Applications, update each one as it moves from applied to interviewing, offered, rejected, or withdrawn.

3. Review the dashboard

The dashboard turns your records into a simple visual summary so you can spot patterns quickly.

Tip: The score is meant to help you reflect, not judge yourself. A higher number means the opportunity feels like a stronger fit for you.
